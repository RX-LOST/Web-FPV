Help & Usage Guide

Control Modes

  • Gamepad — Plug in a controller and go. Left stick drives, right stick steers.
  • PC Mode — Drive with keyboard and mouse. W/S accelerates, mouse steers — great for quick testing at your desk.
  • Joysticks — On-screen virtual joysticks that pop up on mobile. Left drives, right turns.

Only one mode can be active at a time — switching to a new one shuts down the old.

PC Mode

  • Hold Shift and scroll your wheel to tweak max forward speed without opening the menu
  • The FWD/REV percentages in the top bar show your current throttle ceiling at a glance

Fine-Tuning

  • Steering Trim — Car pulling to one side? Nudge this to straighten it out
  • Deadzone — Stops jittery center inputs from sending phantom commands
  • Servo Trim +/− — Micro-adjustments for your servo's neutral position

Video Settings

  • Drop the resolution if the stream feels sluggish — lower res means snappier response
  • Expect a ~2 second video blink when switching resolutions while the stream reboots

Troubleshooting

  • No video — Double-check the webcam is plugged in and run systemctl status ustreamer on the Pi
  • No controls — Make sure the status dot is green. For gamepads, check the Bluetooth connection and drivers.
  • Frozen video — Red overlay means the stream dropped. It'll try to reconnect on its own
  • Laggy — Drop the resolution or FPS, and close any extra browser tabs hogging bandwidth
